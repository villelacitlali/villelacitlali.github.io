Renewable Energies

Solar Energy

Solar energy is harnessed from the sun's radiation. It is captured using solar panels and converted into electricity or heat. It can replace coal and gas power.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is generated from the wind using turbines that convert kinetic energy into electricity. It can replace coal and gas power.

Hydropower

Hydropower, or hydroelectric energy, is derived from the movement of water, typically in rivers and dams, and used to generate electricity. It can replace coal and gas power.

Biomass Energy

Biomass energy comes from organic materials, such as plant and animal waste. It can be used to produce electricity, heat, and biofuels, replacing coal, oil, and gas power.

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy is the heat from within the Earth. It is used for electricity generation and direct heating applications. It can replace coal and gas power.
